Ring Sizing Tips

Best Time to Measure

Measure your ring size at the end of the day when your fingers are at their largest.

Temperature Matters

Cold weather can shrink your fingers by up to half a size, while heat can expand them.

Consider Ring Width

Wide rings (6mm+) typically require a size larger than narrow rings for comfort.

Double Check

Measure multiple times and use different existing rings to ensure accuracy.
